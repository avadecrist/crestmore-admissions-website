## Scene 1 — Admissions Office

[00:13]

Well, what did Sarah say?

Basically the same thing she said to you.

So you're stuck with all the interviews?

Yep. Three back-to-back today.

Yikes.

And she said I need to be less harsh with my reviews, but it's not my fault these kids are freaks. That's why I don't do interviews.

I hear you. Try to have an open mind. I can give you some—

## Scene 2 — Interview Room

[00:40]

*(Footsteps approaching. Door closes. Computer typing.)*  

**Sam:**  
Let's see who we're dealing with today.

*(Computer launches virtual interview.)*  

**Sam:**  
Hey.

**Emily:**  
Hello.

**Sam:**  
You must be Emily.

**Emily:**  
That's me.

**Sam:**  
Great. I'm Sam. I'll be interviewing you today. You can go ahead and introduce yourself and we'll go from there.

**Emily:**  
Okay, my name is Emily and I'm a senior at Rocky Mountain High School. I'm currently taking six AP classes. I'm the president of the Sustainable Solutions Club and I'm the captain of the girls soccer team.

**Sam:**  
Wow, that was great.

## Scene 3 — Interview Continues

[01:11]

**Emily:**  
Oh, and I, um, I'm very interested in your mole- molecular biology program.

**Sam:**  
That's great to hear. And what specifically interests you about molecular biology?

**Emily:**  
Well…

**Emily:**  
I'm interested in doing clinical research.

**Sam:**  
I'm sorry?

**Emily:**  
Specifically cancer cell research to determine the benefits of stem cell transplants during treatment.

**Sam:**  
Uh, that's great.

*(Emily’s microphone clicks repeatedly.)*  

**Sam:**  
You don't need to keep muting and unmuting. You can stay unmuted.

**Emily:**  
Okay.

**Sam:**  
Oh — I think you muted yourself again.

---

## Scene 4 — Interview Questions

[01:48]

**Sam:**  
But that's great. What specifically do you like about Crestmore?

**Emily:**  
What I love most about Crestmore is that you focus on creating whole and well-rounded students, specifically through the four pillars of success: social, physical, intellectual, and spiritual.

**Sam:**  
Uh… wow.

**Emily:**  
I also love the size of Crestmore. 7,500 undergraduate and 2,500 graduate students is perfect.

**Sam:**  
Definitely.

---

## Scene 5 — Suspicion

[02:14]

*(A faint voice whispers off-screen.)*  

**John:**  
Sit up straighter. You need to act professional.

**Sam:**  
I'm sorry, what was that?

**John:**  
Are you not muted?

**Emily:**  
Dad, stop!

**Sam:**  
Hello? Is there someone else there with you?

**Emily:**  
No, no. You must be hearing my family that's downstairs.

---

## Scene 6 — The Interview Unravels

[02:26]

**Sam:**  
It is very important that you're completing this interview independently, without help from a parent.

**Emily:**  
Of course. Crestmore is one of my top choices. I take this interview very seriously.

**Sam:**  
Okay then. Can you tell me what you want to be involved in at Crestmore?

**Emily:**  
What a great question. Hmm...

*(A whisper interrupts again.)*  

**John:**  
Emily… smile.

**Emily:**  
I heard you have an environmental impact club and I'd love to join that and continue my work in sustainability.

**Sam:**  
That's awesome.

**Emily:**  
Yeah, um, I'd also love to continue playing soccer — probably just on an intramural team.

**John:**  
Intramural? We talked about this.

## Scene 7 — The Interview Breaks Down

[03:00]

*(Emily’s microphone is muted.)*  

**Sam:**  
Emily, please unmute yourself.

*(Sam waits impatiently.)*  

**Sam:**  
Emily, I heard that. Just unmute yourself. Emily, I can see your mouth moving. I know you're talking to someone.

**Emily:**  
I'm so sorry. I meant to say that I want to play on the competitive team, and that I've been in touch with the coach.

**Emily:**  
Uh… coach…

*(A whisper cuts in again.)*  

**John:**  
Johnson. Coach Johnson.

**Emily:**  
Coach Johnson. Yeah.

**Sam:**  
Unfortunately, I've heard someone in the background of your interview multiple times now, Emily, which I explained is not allowed. We're going to have to cut this interview short.

**Emily:**  
No, please no. I promise I can finish the interview by myself.

**Sam:**  
I'm afraid we're almost out of time, and I'm going to have to report you for the misconduct.

---

## Scene 8 — The Father Reveals Himself

[03:35]

*(A new voice steps in.)*  

**John:**  
Stop. It was me. I'm Emily's father.

**Sam:**  
Sir, we're going to have to end the interview. This is completely unprofessional and inappropriate.

**Emily:**  
Can I please have another chance?

**Sam:**  
I'm afraid—

**John:**  
Mrs. Sam, I'd apologize for my behavior. My daughter here is truly incredible, and I just wanted her to put her best foot forward.

**John:**  
Is it so bad that we care so much about her chances of getting into Crestmore? God forbid a father knows what's best for his daughter and wants to help her accomplish it.

**Sam:**  
Sir…

**John:**  
Crestmore has been my dream. I—her dream since she was a young girl.

**Emily:**  
Dad, stop.

**John:**  
Sweetie, let me handle this.

---

## Scene 9 — The Speech

[04:11]

*(John launches into a dramatic appeal.)*  

**John:**  
Emily fulfills all of Crestmore's pillars. She is an athlete — physical. She is a straight-A, with only two B's and one C, student — intellectual. An avid churchgoer who prays most nights — spiritual. And she has many friends — social.

**John:**  
I am confident that my daughter is the perfect fit for Crestmore, and I ask — no, I beg — that you give her another chance.

**John:**  
Not for my sake, not for her sake, but for the future of Crestmore.

*(A long pause.)*  

---

## Scene 10 — Sam Ends It

[04:40]

**Sam:**  
Is that all?

**John:**  
That is all.

**Sam:**  
Well, we still cannot continue this interview.

**John:**  
Ma'am, I kindly ask for your grace. I am just an eager parent.

**Sam:**  
It was great meeting you both. I will be ending the call now.

**John:**  
Ma'am—

**Sam:**  
Emily, it was nice to meet you.

**Emily:**  
Thank you. I'm so sorry.

**John:**  
Ma'am, we will prosecute. I didn't want it to come to this, but—

**Emily:**  
Just end the call, please.

**Sam:**  
Sure. Goodbye, Emily. Goodbye, sir.

**Emily:**  
Bye.

*(Call disconnects.)*
